ABOUT US

Discosapien is more than your average DJ entertainment company. When booking talent, we only choose DJs with top-level mixing skills and a passion for performing at all types of events. Instead of other disc jockey companies who just play song to song on their iPod, we’ll keep the party going all night long. Whatever your occasion may be, our Denver DJs will always give you live mash-ups and a completely interactive experience. From weddings and corporate events to school dances and more, we’ve got you covered! In addition to entertaining guests of all ages at various events throughout the Colorado area, Discosapien also has a DJ music production school, recording studio, silent headphone disco parties and photo booth rentals. If you want to take your music to the next level and release your own tracks, we invite you to check out our EDM record label. Whether you want to hire our Denver DJs for your next event or learn from the best of the best, we can’t wait to hear from you! Contact us at 303-981-0429 to get started.
